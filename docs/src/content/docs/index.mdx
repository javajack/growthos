## TL;DR

GrowthOS is a **unified growth platform** that replaces 5+ disconnected SaaS growth tools — waitlists, referrals, lifecycle emails, surveys, NPS, and embeddable components — with a single integrated system. Every module shares one contact graph, one event bus, and one SDK. The result: **compound growth leverage** where each module makes every other module smarter, at a fraction of the cost of a stitched-together stack.

- **One identity** across every touchpoint — no more "same person, three records"
- **One SDK** integration — under 30 minutes, not 20-40 engineer-weeks
- **One price** — $49-$149/mo, not $200-$2,000/mo across 6-10 vendors

## What You Get

<CardGrid>
  <Card title="Unified Contact Graph" icon="document">
    A single identity for every user across every module. Waitlist sign-ups, referral participants, survey respondents, and email recipients all resolve to one profile — automatically. No more CSV merging or brittle Zapier chains.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Referral Engine" icon="rocket">
    Two-sided referral campaigns with automatic reward tracking, fraud detection, and attribution. Referrals feed directly into lifecycle emails and NPS segmentation — zero custom wiring required.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Lifecycle Emails" icon="email">
    Event-driven email sequences triggered by real user behavior: sign-ups, referrals, NPS responses, feature adoption milestones. Built-in templates for onboarding, activation, retention, and win-back flows.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Viral Waitlist" icon="list-format">
    Priority-ranked waitlists with built-in referral mechanics. Every waitlist position improves when a user refers others — turning your launch queue into a growth loop that compounds before you even ship.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Surveys & NPS" icon="star">
    In-app and email-based surveys with NPS, CSAT, and custom question types. Responses feed directly into contact profiles, enabling automated follow-up: detractor rescue emails, promoter referral prompts, and real-time Slack alerts.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Embeddable Components" icon="puzzle">
    Drop-in React/Vue/Web Component widgets for referral cards, waitlist forms, NPS prompts, and survey modals. Ship growth features in minutes with pre-built, customizable UI that matches your brand.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>
