GrowthOS Phase 3 is the **scale and sophistication** phase. The platform graduates from a collection of powerful modules into a fully orchestrated growth engine with visual journey building, multi-channel delivery (WhatsApp, SMS, Push), deep analytics, and advocacy programs that turn users into ambassadors.

---

## Timeline & Team

| Parameter | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| **Timeline** | Months 10-15 |
| **Team** | 6-10 engineers + 2 designers |
| **Total Engineering** | ~48.5 weeks |

---

## Entry Trigger

<Aside type="caution" title="Phase 3 begins when ALL conditions are met">
- ≥ 100 paying customers
- ≥ 5 modules active per top-quartile tenant
- NPS ≥ 50
- First enterprise inquiry received
</Aside>

---

## Goal

Deliver the **flagship feature moment** — the Journey Builder visual canvas — unlock multi-channel delivery (WhatsApp, SMS, Push), and justify the **$149-$299/mo** price tier. Phase 3 transforms GrowthOS from a toolkit into a platform that enterprises evaluate seriously.
