GrowthOS Phase 2 deepens the platform, expands the module catalog, and positions the product for mid-market SaaS companies (50-200 employees). The goal is to move from early-adopter traction to repeatable, scalable growth.

---

## Timeline & Team

| Parameter | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| **Timeline** | Months 5-9 |
| **Team** | 4-7 engineers + 1 designer |
| **Total Engineering** | ~29.5 weeks |

---

## Entry Trigger

<Aside type="caution" title="Phase 2 begins when ALL conditions are met">
- ≥ 30 paying customers
- ≥ 3 modules active per tenant (average)
- NPS ≥ 40
</Aside>

---

## Goal

Expand into mid-market SaaS (50-200 employees) and reach **$20K-$50K MRR** by delivering platform-level primitives (segments, scoring, webhooks) that make every module smarter and more connected.
